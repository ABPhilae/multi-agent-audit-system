FINDING HK-2024-001
Title: Trade Reconciliation Control Gap
Severity: Critical | Business Area: Global Markets — Fixed Income
Owner: Head of Operations | Target Date: 2026-03-15 | Status: In Progress
Description: Daily reconciliation of OTC derivative trades between the trading
system and the clearing house is performed manually for >50 trades per day.
Risk: undetected discrepancies exceeding HKD 5M. Automated controls failed
during system upgrade in July 2025. No compensating controls in place.
HKMA Reference: SPM TM-G-1 Section 4.2 (Operational Risk Controls)
Remediation: Automate reconciliation; implement 4-eyes check for manual entries.
